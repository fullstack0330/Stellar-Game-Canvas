STELLAR DRIFT

INSTRUCTIONS:
Can you reach a score of 10,000 by dodging oncoming asteroids?
The speed and colour scheme will constantly change as you gain more points.

CONTROLS:
MOBILE: Control the space craft by touching the left and right side of the screen.
COMPUTER: Use the left and right arrow keys.
